Boiled Egg

Description

This is a simple way to make boiled eggs. It is fast and delicious at the same time! Perfect for a simple breakfast!

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pot

Steps

  1. Boil pot of water on the stove
  2. Add egg to pot and cook for 4 minutes
  3. Remove egg and place in ice water
  4. Salt the egg
  5. Enjoy!
